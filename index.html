What We Do

Comprehensive Water & Landscape Solutions

From underground irrigation to landscape illumination — we handle every system that keeps your property thriving.

Sprinkler Systems

Custom-designed automatic sprinkler systems with Hunter and Rain Bird heads. Precision coverage zones, smart controllers, and efficient water distribution for every inch of your lawn.

Irrigation Design

Engineered irrigation layouts tailored to your property’s soil, slope, and plant needs. Drip systems, rotary heads, and zone mapping for maximum water efficiency.

Drainage Solutions

French drains, channel drains, dry wells, and grading solutions that eliminate standing water, protect foundations, and prevent landscape erosion.

Landscape Lighting

Low-voltage LED path lights, accent uplights, and architectural illumination. Transform your property after dark with energy-efficient outdoor lighting systems.

Winterization & Spring Start-Up

Professional compressed-air blowouts to protect your investment from Michigan winters. Spring activation, head adjustment, and full system diagnostics included.

Maintenance & Repair

Broken heads, leaking valves, controller issues — we diagnose and fix it fast. Scheduled maintenance plans keep your system running at peak performance year-round.

Our Process

From Consultation to Green Lawn

A proven 5-step process that takes the complexity out of irrigation.

1

Free On-Site Consultation

We walk your property, assess soil conditions, identify problem areas, and discuss your goals. No pressure, no obligation.

2

Custom System Design

We engineer a zone-by-zone irrigation plan optimized for your property’s unique layout, water pressure, and plant needs.

3

Professional Installation

Our crew handles everything — trenching, piping, head placement, wiring, and controller setup. Minimal disruption to your existing landscape.

4

Testing & Calibration

Every zone tested for coverage, pressure, and timing. We fine-tune spray patterns and program your controller before we leave.

5

Ongoing Support

Seasonal maintenance, winterization, spring start-ups, and emergency repairs. We’re in this for the long haul.
